Lunges

Description:Lunges are one of the fundamental exercises targeting the lower body, particularly the quadriceps, hamstrings, and glutes. They can be performed with weights or using body weight alone.

Benefits:

  1. Strengthens lower body muscles:Targets multiple muscles in the legs and glutes, improving strength and endurance
  2. Improves balance and stability:The lunge movement requires balance, engaging core muscles.
  3. Increases flexibility:Enhances flexibility in the hips and thighs
  4. Corrects muscle imbalances:Focuses on each leg individually,helping to reduce muscle discrepancies
