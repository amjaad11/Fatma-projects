Side Plank Exercise - Modified

Step 1

Starting Position: Lie on your right side on an exercise mat with your left leg directly over your right leg. Bend your knees to a comfortable position. Support your upper body on your right arm with your elbow bent to 90 degrees, positioned directly under your shoulder. Align your head with your spine and keep your hips and lower knee on the mat.

Step 2

Upward Phase: Exhale as you gently contract your abdominal/core muscles to stabilize your spine. Lift your hips off the mat, keeping contact with your knee and head aligned with your spine.

Step 3

Lowering Phase: Inhale as you gently return to your starting position, maintaining control throughout the movement.

Step 4

Exercise Variation: To increase the intensity, hold the raised position for longer periods.
