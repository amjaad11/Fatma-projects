Leg Press:
The Leg Press exercise is a workout focused on strengthening the leg muscles, such as the quadriceps, hamstrings, and glutes, by pushing weights with the legs, enhancing muscle strength and endurance.

Benefits:

  1. Strengthens Leg Muscles: Primarily helps in building lower body strength.
  2. Improves Strength and Endurance: Enhances physical power and stamina.
  3. Suitable for Many Individuals: An excellent option for people with injuries or knee issues, as it allows leg training without excessive strain
