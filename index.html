Squats:
A squat is a strength training exercise that primarily targets the muscles of the legs and hips. It is considered one of the foundational movements in fitness and bodybuilding, involving lowering the body into a sitting position and then returning to standing. Squats can be performed with body weight or with added resistance, such as weights.

Benefits of Squats:

  1. Strengthens Muscles: Targets the quadriceps, glutes, and hamstrings
  2. Improves Balance: Enhances body stability and balance
  3. Increases Strength: A compound exercise that engages multiple muscle groups at once, helping to build overall strength
  4. Enhances Athletic Performance: Beneficial for athletes in various sports, improving movement capabilities.
  5. Burns Calories: Aids in burning fat and calories, contributing to weight management
