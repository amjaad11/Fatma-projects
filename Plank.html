plank
The Plank is an isometric core exercise that involves holding a push-up-like position while keeping the body straight from head to heels. This exercise targets the core muscles, including the rectus abdominis, transverse abdominis, and obliques, along with engaging the shoulders, back, and legs for stability.
Benefits of Plank:
  1. Strengthens Core Muscles: Targets the entire core, improving overall stability and strength
  2. Improves Posture: Reinforces proper alignment of the spine and strengthens the muscles supporting it.
  3. Boosts Balance and Coordination: Engages multiple muscle groups, enhancing overall body control.
  4. Enhances Athletic Performance: Builds a solid foundation for movements requiring core stability.
  5. Prevents Back Pain: Strengthens the lower back muscles, reducing strain on the spine.
  6. Accessible to All Levels: Simple to perform and requires no equipment, making it suitable for beginners and advanced athletes alike.
